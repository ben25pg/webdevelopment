    • A tribute to Dr APJ Abdul kalam

    The Beginning..

    Born-October 15,1931 in Rameshwaram,India.

    "Birthday:The only day in your life...your mother smiled when you cried"
    -Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam.

    Education

    AS a bright and hardworking student, he grduated in physics from Saint Joseph College, Tiruchirapalli and moved to Madras in 1955 to study Aerospace Engineering in Madras Institute of Technology.

    As a Scientist..

    As the Project Director of India's first Satellite launch Vehicle(SLV III) successfully deployed the Rohini satellite in earth's orbit. In 1982, rejoined DRDO and produced a number od succesful missiles, hence earned the nickname "The Missile Man".

    Awards

    Kalam was Honoured by Government of India with Padma Bhushan in 1981 and Padma Vibhushan in 1990.Recevied 7 honorary doctorate from 40 universities. Ranked number 2 in Outlook India's poll of The Greatest Indian in 2012.

    The 11th Indian President

    Sworn in as the President of India on July 25, 2002.Affectionately known as The People's President. Later told in an interview that "signing the office of profit bill was the toughest decision during his tenure." Kalam later then became a visiting professor due to his love for teaching.

    The Exit..

    Abdul Kalam believed that there is no other profession in the world that is more important to society than that of a teacher. On 27 July 2015, only five minutes into his lecture at Indian Institute of Management Shillong kalam collapsed and the era of The Missile Man of India came to an End.
